Hi! I'm Marie Zendee Patalinghug, a passionate UI/UX Designer, Graphic Artist, and Frontend Developer based in the Philippines. With a strong eye for detail and love for clean design, I blend creativity with functionality to craft beautiful, user-centered digital experiences.
